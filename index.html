How to Get Started with United pay

1.Link Account:

Link your account by providing your basic details (email, password, business name, etc.). This will be your personal dashboard where you manage your payments, transactions, and settings.

2. Verify Your Identity

To ensure security and comply with financial regulations, we’ll ask you to verify your identity. You may need to upload some documents, like your ID or business license, to complete this process.

3. Connect Your Bank Account

Link your bank account or payment method to start receiving payments. Our platform supports multiple payment options, ensuring you can accept funds directly into your account without hassle.

4. Choose Your Payment Plan

Pick a payment plan that suits your business needs. Whether you're a freelancer, a startup, or a large enterprise, we offer flexible plans with competitive rates.

5. Set Up Your Payment Methods

Customize the payment options you’d like to offer your customers. We support credit/debit cards, e-wallets, and alternative payment methods to give your clients flexibility.

6. Integrate Payment System

Easily integrate our payment gateway into your website or app with our simple plugins or APIs. Our documentation will guide you through every step, so you can start accepting payments right away.

7. Start Accepting Payments

Once set up, you’re ready to begin receiving payments! You can manage transactions from your dashboard, track progress, and get detailed reports.

8. Get Support When You Need It

If you need help at any point, our customer support team is just a click away. We offer live chat, email, and phone support to assist you in setting up or resolving any issues.
